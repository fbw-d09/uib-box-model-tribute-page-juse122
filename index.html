Orianna

The Lady of Clockwork

"Time tick-ticks away."
  • Initially born between 946 AN and 976 AN as Orianna Reveck, the daughter of Corin Reveck
  • Resident of Piltover
  • Got her whole body replaced by artificial parts after grave illness
  • Released on June 1st, 2011
  • Safe and versatile mid lane mage champion that fits into lots of team comps
